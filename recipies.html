Recipies

22 Korean-Kimchi Jjigae
Have some old kimchi in the fridge? Make this kimchi jjigae! It’s delicious with fatty pork. Such a comfort food!
22 Korean-Samgyeopsal
Samgyeopsal aka grilled pork belly is a Korean BBQ meal that is affordable, meaty, savory, sand a great dinner for friends and family.
22 Korean-Bokkeumbap
Kimchi fried rice (kimchi bokkeumbap, 김치볶음밥) is a humble Korean dish made basically with kimchi and leftover rice.
22 Brazilian-One Pot Shrimp in Coconut Sauce
A 30-minute, creamy, and comforting One Pot Shrimp in Coconut Sauce with blended vegetables to serve over rice or noodles. It is dinner made easy!
22 Indian-Paneer Tikka Masala
Pan fry pieces of paneer until golden brown and then bathe them in a rich tomato sauce. The paneer tikka masala is so good that you’ll want to lick the bowl!
22 Mexican-Churros
These fried cinnamon-sugar goodies are best when fresh and hot. Try them with a cup of coffee or hot chocolate. Don't be surprised if people start dunking...and then go back for more fritters.
